Paying for your project



We charge for the design and development of websites at various stages. Before we start work, a 50% deposit of the total sum due is payable (the deposit). When the websites design is approved by you and the home page is completed, a further 25% is due (design payment). The final 25% is payable when the website is complete and ready to 'go-live' (final payment) Your website will only be switched to 'live' mode when it has been paid for, in full. The initial deposit payment is non-refundable after CO Design Solutions has committed resource or completed research and/or design work on the client's behalf. CO Design Solutions Ltd will charge 1.9% fee for all payments made using a credit or debit card. This fee is charged due to the costs incurred by CO Design Solutions for receiving such payments by our service provider. If this is not acceptable to the client, we also accept BACS and cheques as alternative payment methods which will incur no payment fees. If paying by cheque, please understand that we will have to allow the cheque to clear the banking system before starting or continuing with your project. CO Design Solutions Ltd own all design and code of the website until final payment has been received in full. On receipt of final payment full ownership of the site files are handed to the client, this includes all design, branding and code.
